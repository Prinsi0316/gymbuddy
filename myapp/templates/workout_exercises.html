Workout Exercises

Muscle Gaining

Exercise Description
Bench Press A compound exercise that works the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
Deadlifts Great for building overall strength, targeting multiple muscle groups.
Barbell Squats Strengthens the legs, core, and helps build muscle mass.
Dumbbell Shoulder Press Targets the shoulders and helps improve upper body strength.
Leg Press Isolates the legs and helps in gaining muscle mass.

Strength Training

Exercise Description
Squats A fundamental exercise for developing leg strength and stability.
Pull-Ups Effective for building upper body strength, targeting the back and biceps.
Push-Ups Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps; great for bodyweight strength.
Plank A core strengthening exercise that builds endurance in both the abs and back.
Deadlifts Full-body exercise that primarily targets the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings.

Weight Gain

Exercise Description
Barbell Rows Helps in developing back muscles and overall mass.
Overhead Press Builds shoulder muscles and increases upper body mass.
Chest Fly Targets the chest muscles, helping in muscle growth.
Leg Curl Isolates the hamstrings, contributing to muscle gain.
Dumbbell Lunges Works multiple muscle groups in the legs for balanced growth.

Weight Loss

Exercise Description
Running Effective cardio for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.
Cycling A low-impact exercise that can burn calories while being easy on the joints.
Jump Rope Great for cardiovascular fitness and burning calories quickly.
HIIT Combines short bursts of intense exercise with periods of rest.
Swimming Full-body workout that burns calories and builds endurance.

Fitness

Exercise Description
Yoga Enhances flexibility and mental focus while reducing stress.
HIIT High-intensity interval training improves overall fitness in a short time.
CrossFit A high-intensity workout combining strength and conditioning.
Pilates Focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body conditioning.
Dance Classes Fun way to improve cardiovascular health while enjoying music.
