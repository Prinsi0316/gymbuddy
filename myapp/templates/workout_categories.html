WORKOUT CATEGORIES

HIIT

A full-body workout that uses high-intensity interval training to improve cardiovascular fitness and muscular strength and endurance.

Squats

A compound exercise that works multiple muscles and joints, and can be progressed to make more difficult as you get stronger.

Circuit Training

A popular body conditioning class that usually involves a series of different activities, such as running and lifting weights.

Strength Training

Builds and strengthens muscles, and can be beneficial for all fitness levels.

Yoga

A type of resistance training that uses your body weight to perform poses and stretches, and also helps improve your balance.

Stretching

Improves flexibility and overall fitness, and includes techniques to safely and effectively improve your movement.

Pilates

A whole body workout that includes leg work, glute work, and upper body strength to improve posture and alignment.

Cardio

Improves the function of your heart, lungs, and circulatory system, and can include exercises like boxing, jogging, and riding a bike.
